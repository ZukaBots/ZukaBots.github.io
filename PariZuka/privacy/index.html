Privacy Policy for PariZuka

This Privacy Policy outlines how PariZuka, a private YouTube chat bot, handles information. Developed by Ameya Gurjar, PariZuka is a non-profit project primarily for personal use by Ameya Gurjar and a select group of friends. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Introduction to PariZuka

PariZuka is a YouTube chat bot built using Node.js, designed to interact with YouTube chat functionalities. It leverages the YouTube Data API v3 for all its operations and integrates Gemini Flash for enhanced chat capabilities.

2. Data Collection and Usage

PariZuka does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users or their chats.

Our operational principle is simple: any data (such as chat messages or user IDs) is used solely and instantaneously to generate a response within the YouTube chat environment. This data is not logged, saved, or retained after the immediate interaction has concluded.

Specifically:

  • No Personal Information: We do not ask for, collect, or store any personally identifiable information about you, such as your name, email address, or location.
  • No Chat Logs: Your chat messages are processed in real-time to formulate a response and are immediately discarded. We do not maintain any logs of your conversations.
  • No User Data Profiles: We do not create profiles of users based on their interactions or any other data.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to share with any third parties.

3. How PariZuka Interacts with Data

When PariZuka operates, it temporarily accesses information directly from the YouTube chat to provide a relevant response. This process is ephemeral:

  • YouTube Data API v3: PariZuka utilizes the YouTube Data API v3 to read incoming chat messages and send responses. Our use of the YouTube API services adheres to the YouTube API Services Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Gemini Flash Integration: The Gemini Flash integration assists in understanding and generating more nuanced chat responses. Similar to the YouTube chat data, any information processed by Gemini Flash is used for immediate response generation and is not retained.

4. Non-Profit and Non-Commercial Nature

PariZuka is a non-profit project. It was developed by Ameya Gurjar and is used by a small, private group of friends. There is no intention to monetize this bot through advertising, subscriptions, data sales, or any other commercial means. Its sole purpose is for personal and private use within our small community.

5. Data Security

While PariZuka does not collect or store data, we understand the importance of security. The bot is developed with standard security practices within the Node.js environment to protect its operational integrity. As there is no user data collected, the primary security focus is on ensuring the bot's stable and private operation.

6. Open Source Status

Currently, PariZuka is not open source. It remains a private project for the use of its developer and friends.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page. As this is a private bot, significant changes will be communicated directly to the limited user group.
