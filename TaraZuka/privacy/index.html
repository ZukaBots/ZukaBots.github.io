Privacy Policy for TaraZuka Bot

Last Updated: 08th July, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to TaraZuka, a Discord bot developed by Ameya Gurjar for ZukaCity. This privacy policy explains how TaraZuka collects, uses, and protects your information. By using TaraZuka, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection and Use

TaraZuka collects the following types of information:

  • Text Messages: TaraZuka reads text messages in channels where it has permission. This is necessary for moderation purposes.
  • Direct Messages (DMs): TaraZuka may send DMs to users when they are kicked or banned from a server.
  • User Data: This includes usernames, user IDs, and server IDs to manage and log moderation actions.

3. Use of Google’s Gemini AI

TaraZuka utilizes Google’s Gemini AI for generating AI responses. By using TaraZuka, you acknowledge that your interactions may be processed by Google’s Gemini AI. Google’s privacy policy can be found here.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously. TaraZuka implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

TaraZuka does not share or disclose your information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law.
  • To protect and defend our rights and property.
  • With your explicit consent.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the moderation services and as required by applicable laws. Data that is no longer needed will be securely deleted.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to certain conditions.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or TaraZuka’s data practices, please contact us at zukacitybots@gmail.com.
